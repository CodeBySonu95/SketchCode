Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 28, 2026

At SketchCode, your privacy is our top priority. As a free platform for developers, we are committed to protecting the limited information we collect while providing you with high-quality source codes and projects.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Name and Email address provided via Google Sign-in to maintain your user profile.
  • Device Information: Device model, OS version, and non-personal identifiers used strictly for app security and analytics.
  • Usage Data: Interaction logs to help us improve app features and performance.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the SketchCode Store features.
  • To personalize your experience on our platform.
  • To enhance security and prevent unauthorized access or system exploitation.
  • For app maintenance, technical analytics, and security monitoring.

3. Data Protection & Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or distribute your personal data. Information is shared only with trusted core services required to operate the application, such as user authentication.

Since SketchCode is now a free platform, we do not process or store any financial or payment information. Any voluntary support sent to developers occurs on external third-party websites and is not handled by our servers.

4. Third-Party Services

Our app utilizes the following essential services:

  • Google Play Services (User Authentication)
  • Firebase (Database & Storage)

5. User Control & Data Deletion

Users have full control over their account data:

  • You may request account or data deletion at any time.
  • To request deletion, please contact us via the official email provided below.
  • Requests will be processed promptly in accordance with platform policies.

6. Children's Privacy

SketchCode does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe such data has been provided, please contact us immediately for its removal.
